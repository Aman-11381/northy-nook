Our Story

NorthyNook Catering began as a small family operation with a big dream: to create memorable culinary experiences that celebrate life's special moments. Founded by Chef Michael Thompson in 2015, our journey started in a modest kitchen with just three staff members and an unwavering commitment to quality.

What sets us apart is our dedication to using locally-sourced ingredients, preparing everything fresh from scratch, and customizing menus to reflect the unique preferences and dietary needs of our clients.

From intimate gatherings of 20 to grand celebrations of 500+, we've had the privilege of serving thousands of events across the region, earning a reputation for reliability, creativity, and culinary excellence.

Our Culinary Philosophy

  • Local Sourcing - We partner with local farmers and suppliers to bring you the freshest seasonal ingredients.
  • Scratch Cooking - Every sauce, dressing, and pastry is crafted in-house by our talented culinary team.
  • Dietary Inclusivity - We accommodate all dietary requirements with the same attention to flavor and quality.
  • Sustainable Practices - We minimize food waste and use eco-friendly packaging whenever possible.
